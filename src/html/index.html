With MIDIBanks you can intersect MIDI-Device messages and transform them into a variety of other messages.
You can further create banks to reassign the device-controllers multiple times.
Supported message types:
Control-Change, Note-On, Note-Off, Program-Change,
Channel-Pressure, Pitch-Bend, Poly-Pressure, System-Exclusive,
Bank-Switch, Group-Switch, Light-On, Light-Off, Text
